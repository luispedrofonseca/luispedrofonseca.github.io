Portfolio

This is a collection of a few of my best projects for the past several years.
Some of these projects were done through agencies and not directly with the final client.
Some projects are under NDA so please don’t share this page publicly.

The Great Xscape VR

Client: Dell Inc.
Release Date: October 2017
Duration: 6 months
Role: Lead Developer
Technology: Unity, SteamVR, HTC Vive, UNet
Description: An escape room (in space) designed to introduce the players to Dell EMC products while at the same time having fun in a VR experience. This project was divided in 2 phases - first we developed the single player game and later we re-imagined it for multiplayer. A gameplay video of the single player can be found here: goo.gl/b2Cu7u

IBM US Open 2017

Client: Spinifex Group
Release Date: August 2017
Duration: 2 months
Role: Developer
Technology: Unity, WebSockets
Description: An experience designed from the ground-up to reimagine the presentation of the realtime data of the US Open games analysed by IBM Watson.

HCI Hero

Kiney

Client: X-Company
Release Date: N/A
Duration: 2 months
Role: Lead Developer
Technology: Unity, iOS

WonderWall

Client: X-Company
Release Date: N/A
Duration: 6 months
Role: Lead Developer
Technology: Unity, iOS, Android
Description: A research project exploring the possibilities of video- mapping on mobile devices. It used a custom algorithm for video perspective distortion across any number of polygons. It allowed for up to 8 simultaneous video sources even on lower-end devices.
